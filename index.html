Decorate your house with Bonsai

Miniature trees which are carefully managed and fashioned to resemble the splendor and beauty of nature are called bonsai plants. These are grown in tiny containers. The technique of bonsai is indeed an ancient art form that originated in China and Japan and then expanded to other regions of the world. The Japanese terms "bon" and "sai," which together translate to "placed in a tray," are just the origins of the English word "bonsai". They are cultivated and trained to stay small by limiting their root growth, cutting branches and leaves, and wiring the trunk and branches to shape them. Bonsai trees are genetically identical to ordinary trees. The aim of bonsai gardening is to produce a balanced and symmetrical small tree that mimics a wild tree.

Bonsai teaches patience and humility, it reminds us to take life one step at a time and that true beauty takes time to grow.
